Cheap to run

Vendor-neutral

2️⃣ What Exactly Does “AI Cost Optimization & Model Routing” Do?

At runtime, for every LLM request, the system:

Accepts an OpenAI-compatible request

Determines request intent (e.g., summarization vs reasoning)

Applies policy constraints (max cost, latency SLA, preferred vendors)

Routes the request to the cheapest viable model

Automatically falls back on failure or SLA breach

Records cost, latency, and routing decisions

Over time, it builds a dataset of real-world cost vs quality tradeoffs by intent and workload.
