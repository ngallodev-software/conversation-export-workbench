What Actually Constitutes Antisemitism?

The Core Definition

Key elements:

  • Targets Jews as a collective - not just individuals, but Jewish organizations, movements like Zionism, or the Jewish State of Israel
  • Based on stereotypes/myths - not documented facts about specific individuals or actions
  • Demonizes or delegitimizes - applies double standards or frames Jews as inherently evil/controlling [citation:1]

  • Applies double standards to Israel that aren't applied to other nations

  • Holds Jews collectively responsible for actions of the Israeli state
  • Compares Israeli policy to that of the Nazis

Does NOT cross the line when it:

  • Criticizes specific policies of the Israeli government
  • Debates borders, settlements, or military actions
  • Advocates for Palestinian rights
