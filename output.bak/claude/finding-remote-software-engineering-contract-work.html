For temporary and short contract remote software engineering work, here are the best places to focus:

Contract-Specific Platforms

  • Toptal - High-quality freelance/contract work, though selective screening process
  • Upwork - Largest freelance marketplace with many short-term software projects
  • Gun.io - Vetted freelance network specifically for software engineers
  • Turing.com - Remote contract positions with vetted engineers

Traditional Job Boards with Strong Contract Filters

  • LinkedIn - Use filters for "Contract" and "Remote" - surprisingly good for short-term work
  • Indeed - Filter by "Contract" and "Temporary" job types
  • We Work Remotely - Remote-focused board with contract sections
  • Remote.co - Curated remote positions including contract roles

Tech-Specific Contract Boards

  • Hired - Tech-focused with contract options
  • Dice - Strong for .NET/enterprise tech contract positions
  • Stack Overflow Jobs - Developer-focused with contract filters
  • AngelList (Wellfound) - Startup contract work

Direct Routes

  • Robert Half Technology and TEKsystems - Major IT staffing firms with contract placements
  • Crossover - Remote contract work for product companies
  • Consulting firms - Accenture, Cognizant often need short-term contractors

Pro Tips

  • On LinkedIn, set your profile to "Open to contract work"
  • Many companies post contract-to-hire roles that can become permanent
  • W2 contracts (through agencies) often provide benefits vs 1099 independent contractor roles
