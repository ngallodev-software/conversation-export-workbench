For contract and temporary remote software engineering work, here are the most effective places to look:

Contract-focused platforms:

  • Toptal - High-end contracts, rigorous vetting process, but matches well with your senior experience
  • Gun.io - Vetted freelance developers, focuses on longer-term contracts (3-12 months)
  • Upwork - Largest freelance marketplace, filter for "Fixed-price" or "Hourly" contracts
  • Freelancer.com - Similar to Upwork but different client base

Tech-specific contract boards:

  • Dice - Strong for tech contracts, filter by "Contract" and "Remote"
  • CyberCoders - Often has 3-6 month contract-to-hire roles
  • Hired - Matches you with companies seeking contractors
  • Turing.com - Remote-first platform connecting engineers with US companies

General job boards with contract filters:

  • LinkedIn - Filter by "Contract" and "Remote" in job type
  • Indeed - Use keywords like "contract," "temporary," "6-month contract"
  • We Work Remotely - Has a contract section
  • Remote.co - Filter for contract positions

For shorter gigs (days to weeks):

  • Fiverr - More suited for smaller, defined projects
  • PeoplePerHour - Project-based work

Staffing agencies specializing in tech contracts:

  • Robert Half Technology
  • TEKsystems
  • Insight Global
  • Modis

Given your .NET/C# and cloud expertise, I'd particularly recommend Toptal, Dice, and reaching out to Robert Half Technology - they frequently place senior .NET engineers in 3-6 month contracts that can extend or convert to permanent roles.
